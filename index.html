Mechanics:

  • Webpage: Launching the webpage where NFT holders can connect Phantom wallet and look at their NFTs value via our algorithm.

  • Solana Liquidity pool: The liquidity pool will be assembled from deposits. People who open the deposit will receive interest from it.

  • Borrow: People can borrow money by using their NFTs as collateral. The limit of the loan depends on the value of their NFTs.

  • Debt Default: If borrowers don’t pay the debt, the algorithm will close the position by selling the collateral asset on the market.

  • Interest rate: Borrowers pay % for the loan, depositors receive % for the liquidity provision. The difference between these rates generates profits for the DAO.
